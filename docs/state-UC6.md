## Invariants (reproduce-on-resume)

- **Setup.** Fix minimal generator $W$, $\lvert W\rvert=m(\mathcal F)=k\ge3$, $U'=U\setminus W$. $A=S\sqcup R$; fibre $\mathcal R_S$, co-fibre $\mathcal S(R)$ (union-closed on $W$, $\ni W$); $\phi(S)=2\lvert S\rvert-k$, $\Phi(\mathcal G)=\sum_{S\in\mathcal G}\phi(S)$. UC5: defect retraction $\bar\jmath_R(S)=\max(\mathcal S(R)\cap2^S)$ (interior operator on $\mathcal S(R)^\uparrow$, fixed points $=\mathcal S(R)$), deficiency $\delta_R(S)=S\setminus\bar\jmath_R(S)$, $\mathcal D(R)=\mathcal S(R)^\uparrow\setminus\mathcal S(R)$, fibre-ideals $I_{R,S_0}\subseteq2^{W_0}$ ($W_0=W\setminus S_0$), $a_{S_0}=\lvert I_{R,S_0}\rvert$, $\Sigma(I)=\sum_{T\in I}\lvert T\rvert$.
- **Flow identity (Thm 1.1).** $\Phi(\mathcal D(R))=\mathrm{Ret}(R)+2\,\mathrm{Def}(R)$, $\mathrm{Ret}(R)=\sum_{S\in\mathcal D(R)}\phi(\bar\jmath_R(S))=\sum_{S_0}(a_{S_0}-1)\phi(S_0)$, $\mathrm{Def}(R)=\sum_{S\in\mathcal D(R)}\lvert\delta_R(S)\rvert=\sum_{S_0}\Sigma(I_{R,S_0})\ge0$. Proof key: $\phi(S)=\phi(\bar\jmath_R(S))+2\lvert\delta_R(S)\rvert$.
- **Tiling-coupling explicit form (Thm 1.3).** $I_{R,S_0}=2^{W_0}\setminus(\mathcal S(R)/S_0)^\uparrow$, where $\mathcal S(R)/S_0=\{T'\setminus S_0:T'\in\mathcal S(R),T'\not\subseteq S_0\}$. Every fibre-ideal of one $R$ determined by the single co-fibre $\mathcal S(R)$. $\bar\jmath_R^{-1}(W)=\{W\}$ always.
- **Boundary conservation law (Thm 1.4 + Lemma 1.4a).** $B(S_0)=(k-\lvert S_0\rvert)a_{S_0}-2\Sigma(I_{R,S_0})\ge0$ = inter-fibre Hasse-edge count of fibre $S_0$; $B(S_0)\ge1$ for $S_0\ne W$. $\Phi(\mathcal D(R))=\sum_{S_0}[a_{S_0}\lvert S_0\rvert-\phi(S_0)]-B(R)=\sum_{S\in\mathcal S(R)^\uparrow}\lvert\bar\jmath_R(S)\rvert-\Phi(\mathcal S(R))-B(R)$, $B(R)=\sum_{S_0}B(S_0)\ge\lvert\mathcal S(R)\rvert-1$.

The residual is irreducibly a sum-level (cross-fibre / cross-generator) statement.
